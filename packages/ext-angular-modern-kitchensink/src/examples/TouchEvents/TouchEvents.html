Ext JS comes with a multitude of touch events available on components. Included touch events that can be used are:
  • touchstart
  • touchmove
  • touchend
  • dragstart
  • drag
  • dragend
  • tap
  • singletap
  • doubletap
  • longpress
  • swipe
  • pinch (on iOS and Android 3+)
  • rotate (on iOS and Android 3+)
